Pumpkin Whoopie Pies

Discover the delightful world of pumpkin whoopie pies, where scrumptious pumpkin cookies stand alone as delectable singles or transform into heavenly indulgences when generously filled with luscious whoopie pie filling. In just about 40 minutes, you can complete this recipe, resulting in a generous yield of either three dozen individual cookies or a mouthwatering batch of 18 delectable pies. These pumpkin whoopie pies offer versatility that's perfect for any occasion, from a sweet solo snack to a charming addition to your dessert spread. So, whether you're treating yourself to a single cookie or sharing the joy with loved ones, these pies embody the essence of cozy comfort with every bite, celebrating the pumpkin season in the most delightful way.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups packed brown sugar
  • 1 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 ½ cups solid pack pumpkin puree
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 ½ tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • ½ tablespoon ground ginger
  • ½ tablespoon ground cloves
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

    • Filling:

  • 1 egg white
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 cups confectioners' sugar, divided
  • ¾ cup shortening

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease baking sheets.

  2. Make the cookie dough: Combine brown sugar and oil in a bowl. Mix in pumpkin and eggs, beating well. Add flour, cinnamon, ginger, cloves, salt, baking powder, baking soda, and vanilla together into a separate bowl. Fold dry ingredients into wet ingredients and mix to combine.

  3. Drop dough by heaping teaspoons onto the prepared baking sheets.

  4. Bake in the preheated oven for 10 to 12 minutes. Let cookies cool while you make the filling.

  5. To make whoopie pie filling: Beat egg white in a bowl and mix in milk, vanilla, then 1 cup confectioners' sugar. Mix well then beat in shortening and remaining 1 cup confectioners' sugar. Beat until light and fluffy.

  6. Assemble once cookies are cool. Make sandwiches from two cookies filled with whoopie pie filling.
